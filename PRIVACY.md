# Privacy

Vibe in Color works entirely on your Mac.

It does not contain analytics, telemetry, advertising, accounts, or network requests.
The app only:

- reads the theme library bundled inside the app;
- enumerates fonts installed on your Mac so you can preview and select a font face;
- stores your font selections and preview sizes in the app's local preferences; and
- writes the generated theme value to the macOS clipboard when you press the clipboard export button.

Vibe in Color does not transmit theme data, font information, preferences, or clipboard contents anywhere.
